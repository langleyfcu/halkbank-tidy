Our International Funding Team

Structured Finance: Implementation and execution of financing deals in the international debt capital markets (i.e. bond issuances, term loan facilities, public offerings etc.), bilateral financing agreements with the third parties.
  • IFI Relations: Management of relations with the International Financial Institutions such as World Bank, European Investment Bank, French Development Agency, Council of Europe Development Bank etc. Receiving long-term funds for financing SMEs operating in various sectors, with a special focus on investments in energy efficiency, innovation, renewable energy, women enterpreneurship, OIZs, sector clusters and particularly priority development regions.
