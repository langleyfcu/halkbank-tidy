  1. What are the vision, mission and targets of Halkbank?
    Halkbank’s fundamental objectives are to be the country’s leading bank while fulfilling all the requirements of universal banking and being strong in retail products and services as well and to continuously create added value for customers, shareholders, and employees by effectively carrying out all its banking services with an awareness and understanding of its social responsibilities and duties.
     
    Halkbank’s fundamental goals are:

    Maintain a sustainable growth in profitability

    Achieve a high level of customer satisfaction

    Provide customer satisfaction through quality and technological development

    Being the leader bank in the country in SME segment

    Provide job satisfaction for the employees

  6. When is Halkbank’s General Assembly?
    General Assembly is held at the latest in March every year. Announcements of General Assembly are disclosed in Borsa Istanbul PDP (Public Disclosure Platform http://www.kap.gov.tr/en/home.aspx , daily newspaper and in our website.
  7. Where do you disclose information?
    Information and update about the Bank is disclosed in Borsa Istanbul - PDP (Public Disclosure Platform) Media Organs and in our website.
  8. When was Halkbank established?
    Halkbank was established in 1933 in Turkey as a state-owned commercial bank according to the Act No:2284 and began its operations in 1938, originally to provide financial services for the SME’s.
